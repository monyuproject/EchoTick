# EchoTick

EchoTick is a simple clock app that helps you keep track of time with **World Clocks** and a clean, lightweight interface.

## Features

- **World Clock**: add multiple cities/time zones and view them in one place
- **Local preferences**: your selected clocks and app settings are saved on your device
- **Fast and lightweight**: built for quick access and readability

## Editions

EchoTick may be distributed in:

- **Free edition**
  - Shows ads (Google AdMob)

> Note: Feature availability can vary by edition.

## Installation

### From Google Play

1. Open the Play Store.
2. Search for **EchoTick**.
3. Tap **Install**.

## How to use

### Add a world clock (city / time zone)

1. Open the app.
2. Go to the **World Clock** screen.
3. Tap **Add** (or the \+ button).
4. Search/select a city (time zone).
5. The city will appear in your list.

### Remove a world clock

1. Open your **World Clock** list.
2. Long\-press or use the delete/remove action on the city.
3. Confirm removal (if prompted).

### Settings and saved data

- Your app preferences (like selected cities/world clocks) are stored **locally on your device**.
- Clearing app data or uninstalling the app removes this local data.
